## What I'm wrestling with right now

Just shipped [nostr-ux.com](https://nostr-ux.com)---opinionated, evidence-based UX research for building Nostr apps that don't lose users. It's 100+ citations backing patterns for onboarding, discovery, performance, and cross-client consistency. "[Sovereignty without sacrifice](/sovereignty-without-sacrifice/)" in practice: showing that freedom tech can have the better protocol *and* better UX.

Three strategic tensions I'm working through:

**Custody versus sovereignty:** How do you balance commercially desirable solutions (often custodial) with enabling true self-sovereignty? Most users choose convenience. Freedom tech needs to remove that forced choice.

**Timing the revenue engine:** When do you shift from founder-led selling to building scalable revenue systems in Bitcoin companies? Particularly when negative price action affects customer faith and interest. When do you step on the gas? When do you wait?

**Developer-first versus customer-first:** Most Bitcoin infrastructure starts developer-focused. When and how do you make the jump to end-customer go-to-market without losing your technical credibility?
